# Privacy Policy for GSTBubble — Invoice & Calculator

**Effective Date:** [Date]

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **GSTBubble — Invoice & Calculator** ("the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
The App is designed to function entirely offline. 
* **Local Storage:** All data you enter into the App, including business details, client information, invoices, and product data, is stored locally on your device using a secure local database (Hive). 
* **No External Servers:** We do not collect, transmit, upload, or store your data on any external servers or cloud services. 
* **Images:** If you choose to upload a business logo, the image file is saved locally on your device to be rendered on your invoices. It is never uploaded to the internet.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## 4. Permissions
The App may request the following permissions to function correctly:
* **Storage / Photos:** To allow you to pick a logo from your device's gallery and save generated PDF invoices to your device.

## 5. Security
The security of your data is important to us. Since all your data remains on your device, it is secured by your device's built-in security mechanisms (such as PIN, password, or biometrics). We recommend keeping your device secure and regularly backing up your data if needed.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and publishing the new Privacy Policy.
